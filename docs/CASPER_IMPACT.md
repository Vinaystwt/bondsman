# Casper Impact

Bondsman demonstrates a Casper-native accountability layer for autonomous finance agents.

## What Runs Today

- Casper Testnet contracts custody and slash agent bonds.
- Delivery-contradiction and duplicate-claim verifiers settle objective evidence.
- x402 paid quote settlement binds one paid quote to one bonded action.
- Canonical Action 27 shows a paid, challenged, resolved watchdog slash with a signed portable receipt.

## Why It Matters

Casper gains a reusable pattern for agent-originated financial actions: require stake before execution, commit reasoning, verify objective faults, slash on proof, and emit portable receipts other agents can inspect. This turns agent trust from reputation text into programmable economic accountability.

## Near-Term Ecosystem Fit

- RWA invoice and procurement payout controls.
- Treasury automation guardrails.
- Paid-service delivery accountability after x402 settlement.
- Agent reputation and receipts for wallets, indexers, and AI clients.
